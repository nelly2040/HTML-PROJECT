Tribute to Wangari Maathai

Environmental activist and 2004 Nobel Peace Prize Winner

Wangari Maathai (1940-2011) was a Kenyan environmentalist and founder of the Green Belt Movement. She was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2004 for her contribution to sustainable development and democracy.

She authored four books, including Unbowed: A Memoir, and was featured in the documentary Taking Root: the Vision of Wangari Maathai (2008).
